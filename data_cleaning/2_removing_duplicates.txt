

Duplicate data is any record that inadvertently/accidently/unintentionally/ shares data with another record in a Database. 

Duplicate data is easy to spot and it mostly occurs when transferring data between systems.


# How to Remove Duplicate Data ?

    ==> At first, go to "Data" in excel ribbon tab.
    ==> Then, Click on "Remove Duplicates"
    ==> Then, "Remove Duplicates" dialog box will appear.

    ==> By default, all the columns, will be ticked.

    ==> so, finally click on "OK" button.

    A pop up message will appear, saying "n duplicate values found and removed: n unique values remain."
