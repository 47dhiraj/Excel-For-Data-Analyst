
Data cleaning is the process of preparing data for analysis by removing or modifying data that is incorrect, incomplete, irrelevant, duplicated, or improperly formatted. 

This data is usually not necessary or helpful when it comes to analyzing data because it may hinder the process or provide inaccurate results.


NOTE : Before cleaning dataset, always copy the raw dataset to some different location(internet will be safe). 
So that, u may need it later as backup.


# Various techniques for cleaning the data:

    1) Remove Duplicates (i.e repeated record/row).
    2) Change the Case of Alphabets in any column u need.
    3) Filtering, Sorting and fixing errors/correcting.
    4) Trimming Spaces.
    5) Changing currency data to numeirc/number.
    6) Fixing Date issues.
    7) Paste as value.
